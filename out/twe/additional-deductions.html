Car loan interest deduction

If you and your spouse purchase a qualifying new vehicle in , you can deduct $10,000 of the car loan interest.

A qualifying vehicle is a car, minivan, van, sport utility vehicle (SUV), pickup truck, or motorcycle that’s:

  • New (not used)
  • Purchased in
  • For personal use
  • Went through final assembly in the U.S.
  • A gross vehicle weight of less than 14,000 lbs.
  • Manufactured primarily for use on public streets, roads, and highways
  • Secured by a loan on the vehicle

Learn more about car loan interest.
